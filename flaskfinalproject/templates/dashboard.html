Impact of Family History on Age of Lung Cancer Onset

Objective: Examining how the presence of a family history of lung cancer influences the age at which the disease typically starts and the distribution of cases across different age groups. It aims to identify patterns that can help in understanding the impact of genetic predisposition on lung cancer onset, thereby informing targeted screening and preventive strategies to improve early detection and treatment outcomes for high-risk individuals.

Analyzing survival status by age group reveals distinct patterns in health outcomes across different life stages.
